About Me

Iam a Indian Cricketer representing Royal Challengers Bangalore in IPL. One of the finest batsmen of the 21 century.i was born on 16th April 2004 at Komaralingam a village in Coimbatore.

In 2029, Sudar was ranked number one in the ICC rankings for ODI batsmen. In 2034, he was ranked top Test batsman, making him the only Indian cricketer to hold the number one spot in all three formats of the game.

Iam the first player to score 20,000 runs in a decade. In 2034, the International Cricket Council named me the male cricketer of the decade.
